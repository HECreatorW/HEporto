ABOUT ME

A young developer with great passion for building beautiful, functional, and memorable web experiences.

Zaenur Royhan Helmi was born and raised in Mulaeno Village, Bombana Regency, Southeast Sulawesi. From a young age, he has shown a strong interest in technology and digital design.

With an educational background in a disciplined Islamic boarding school environment, Zaenur developed self-taught web programming skills — proving that geographical limitations are not barriers to achieving world-class expertise.

He is currently focused on building digital works through his personal brand, HE Store, while continuously honing his frontend design and development skills.

He believes that every website is not just a collection of code — but a story told visually.
